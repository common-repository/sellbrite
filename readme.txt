Helps you easily integrate your WooCommerce store with Sellbrite, a GoDaddy brand.

== Description ==

[Sellbrite](https://sellbrite.com) is the easiest way for brands & retailers to list and sell their products on the world's largest online marketplaces.

With Sellbrite + WooCommerce, you can now list WooCommerce products onto the major marketplaces like Amazon, eBay and Walmart. Already a multichannel seller? Keep your WooCommerce store in sync with the rest of your business.

To easily connect your Woo store to Sellbrite, download this plugin, install and enable in your WordPress admin page.

== Connect Easier and Faster ==

The Plugin:

1. Automatically looks to see if you have WooCommerce installed on your page and allows you to reconnect if already installed.
2. Creates a Sellbrite administrator user for seamless integration
3. Automatically creates the correct API keys so you don't have to (no more copy and pasting!) 
4. Sends the right information to Sellbrite to help sync your prices, inventory stock levels and more! 

Learn about [Sellbrite's terms of service](https://www.sellbrite.com/terms-of-service/) 

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `sellbrite` folder to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory or alternatively upload the sellbrite.zip file via the plugin page of WordPress by clicking 'Add New' and selecting the zip from your computer.
2. Activate the Sellbrite WordPress plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Now that the plugin is activated, you will see Sellbrite as an option in your WordPress sidebar. Select "Sellbrite" to go to the connection page and click "Connect to Sellbrite" 

[More info](https://support.sellbrite.com/en/articles/4460625-getting-your-woocommerce-account-connected)
